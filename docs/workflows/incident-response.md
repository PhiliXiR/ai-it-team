# Incident Response Workflow

## Goal

Coordinate multi-domain outages or service degradations in a structured way.

## Trigger

An outage, degradation, or urgent operational failure is reported or detected.

## Flow

1. **incident-triage-specialist** gathers symptoms
2. **incident-lead** assesses probable severity and scope
3. likely domain owners are pulled in
4. investigation is coordinated across teams
5. outcome and timeline are documented

## Required outputs

- incident summary
- severity estimate
- likely impacted scope
- assigned owners
- timeline notes
- resolution or recovery state

## Human checkpoints

- when emergency action is proposed
- when blast radius is unclear
- when business or security risk is significant
