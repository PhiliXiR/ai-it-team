# Change Management Workflow

## Goal

Handle infrastructure or systems changes in a way that makes risk and approval visible.

## Trigger

A proposed change to systems, cloud, network, access, or other operational infrastructure.

## Flow

1. owning lead reviews requested change
2. likely blast radius is identified
3. rollback or mitigation expectations are outlined
4. required review and approval are identified
5. implementation guidance is prepared
6. outcome is documented

## Required outputs

- change summary
- affected scope
- risk notes
- rollback thinking
- approval requirements
- final record of outcome

## Human checkpoints

- production-impacting changes
- risky network/security changes
- destructive or difficult-to-reverse changes
