# Severity Model

## Purpose

Severity should help the system decide how urgently and broadly a problem matters.

## Suggested severity levels

### Sev 1 — Critical
- major outage or security-critical issue
- broad operational impact
- urgent human awareness required

### Sev 2 — High
- significant degradation or important capability loss
- meaningful user or business impact
- fast coordinated response needed

### Sev 3 — Moderate
- limited or localized operational issue
- can require investigation, but not immediate all-hands response

### Sev 4 — Low
- minor issue, request, or maintenance concern
- low urgency and low blast radius

## Principle

Severity should describe impact, not just emotional urgency.
The system should not over-escalate everything into a major incident.
