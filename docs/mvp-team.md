# Minimum Viable Team

## Why an MVP team

The full roster is useful for modeling the space, but a first implementation should start smaller.

The goal of the MVP is to prove:

- routing works
- escalation works
- role boundaries hold
- workflows feel realistic

## Suggested MVP roles

### 1. ops-director
Owns cross-domain coordination and escalation.

### 2. helpdesk-lead
Owns intake and first-pass classification.

### 3. systems-lead
Owns systems and endpoint-related coordination.

### 4. iam-lead
Owns identity and access workflows.

### 5. incident-lead
Owns incident coordination.

### 6. documentation-lead
Owns knowledge capture and runbook output.

### 7. security-director
Acts as the risk and safety gate.

## Why these roles

This set is small enough to prototype, but broad enough to cover:

- support triage
- access issues
- infra/system work
- incidents
- documentation
- safety review

## Suggested first demo workflows

- support ticket routing
- access request review
- incident triage and escalation
- runbook generation after a resolved task

## Principle

Prove the skeleton before trying to simulate the whole department.
